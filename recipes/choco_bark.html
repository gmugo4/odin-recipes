Caramel Apple Dark Chocolate Bark

  • 2 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips, divided
  • 1 cup crushed pretzels
  • ¾ cup chopped pecans
  • 1 (11 ounce) package caramel bits (such as Kraft®)
  • 1 (3.4 ounce) package apple chips

Step 1: Place 2 cups of chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl and melt in the microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between, until smooth and consistent.
